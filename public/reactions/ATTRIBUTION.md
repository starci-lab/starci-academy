Mapping (filename ← Fluent asset):
- `like.svg`  ← Thumbs up (default skin tone)
- `love.svg`  ← Red heart
- `haha.svg`  ← Face with tears of joy
- `wow.svg`   ← Face with open mouth
- `sad.svg`   ← Crying face
- `angry.svg` ← Pouting face
